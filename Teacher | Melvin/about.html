Who Am I?

My name's Melvin, I'm from Richmond, Virginia in the United States. I lived in that area for all of my childhood. I went to Manchester High School in Midlothian, Virginia. During that time in my life I started to dream about traveling and working abroad. I didn't know if something like that was probable.

Nonetheless, I received the opportunity to travel to France for a year studying abroad. It was a life changing experience. From that point on I made it my destiny to travel. After working my way through various possible avenues, I couldn't find success. I decided I should take myself more serious and return to school to receive a degree in teaching English.

It wasn't easy nor cheap, but in the end worth it. Because, after teaching for almost two years now and living in Chile, it's the most rewarding job I've ever had.

I've taught online and inside classrooms over this time and I've learned a lot about how to succeed in this field. Teaching is a challenge, but I'm always learning. This website is my endeavor to provide the tools and resources I feel my students would benefit from.

Many of us studied a foreign language a long time ago and now trying to become fluent is easier said than done. But with practice and determination, it can be done.

This website will be a complete work in progress as I continue to learn more about this platform. Please take every opportunity you find here to practice your skills.
